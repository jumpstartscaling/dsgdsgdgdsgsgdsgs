                      Cobalt #21 Data Sheet

       Description: Cobalt alloy #21 is a Molybdenum strengthened, high
       temperature, corrosion resistant alloy with exceptional impact, erosion , and
       metal to metal wear resistance. The alloy work hardens in service. It has
       moderate abrasion resistance as compared to Seaco Cobalt alloy #6.

       Applications: Hot Forming Dies, Forging Dies, Erosion Shields, Valve Seats
       and plugs, Hydro-Turbine Runners, Pump shafts and sleeves.

       Typical Chemical Analysis:
            C     Mn Si      Ni       Cr    W      Mo    Fe    Co
            .25 .50 .60 2.5           26    -      5.5   1.0   Bal.

       Machineability: Good

       Hardness: Rc 22-32 as deposited, Rc 38-40 wok hardened

       Hot Hardness (DPH): 800°F - 130; 1000°F - 135; 1200°F - 140;
                           1400°F - 110

       Adhesive Wear Data (Avg.): 90lb Load - 2.5mm³; 150lb Load - 5.2mm³
       (Volume Loss)           210lb Load - 10.3mm³; 300lb Load - 14.5mm³

       Abrasive Wear Data: Volume Loss of 86.4mm³ on ASTM G65B Test

       Charpy Impact Strength (Un-Notched, Room Temperature): 27 Ft. Lb

       Density: .299 Lbs/In³

       Liquidus: 2480°F
